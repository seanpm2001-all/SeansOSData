October 2019

October 31st 2019

View the update log entry for today

I am officially turning this into a long-term multi-use project now. What all started with me finding a way to backup my current phone to the next one has turned into a big database of device info throughout my life.

Prominent changes that occurred today were the introduction of the update log and developer log, and a new iOS page. I will start adding in other machines and operating systems soon.

Sean Patrick Myrick on October 31st 2019

View the update log entry for today

This project has taken a turn today, and now I have introduced a universe and galaxy system. 9 branches of devices have been listed, the one that I started out with is now considered the Samsung Galaxy (a pun of the Samsung Galaxy line of phones)

There is now a structured directory system, with lots of device info. It is taking me some time to add in all the icons, and some of them are hard to find. A lot of things can still be done.
